What is Photonic Crystal Fiber?

Photonic crystal fibers contain microscopic air holes arranged in a periodic lattice around a solid core. These structures control the propagation of light using photonic bandgap effects.

Key Parameters

  • Pitch (Λ)
  • Air hole diameter (d)
  • Refractive index
  • Wavelength

Applications

  • Optical communications
  • Nonlinear optics
  • Sensors
  • Supercontinuum generation
